STUDENTS MUST ATTEND DAY 1, other days missed must be made up. It is MANDATORY for a parent/mentor(21 or older)who is able to sign off on "Rookie hours" to attend the first day of class for the first hour and a half regardless of students' age. We will go over the curriculum requirements for Maryland Driver's License and the rules & regulations for our course,along with new laws and driving schedule information. We look forward to meeting you.

1. Students must successfully complete 30 hours of classroom work. Students must pass each unit test with an 80% or better. If a student misses 12 hours of instruction, the student must re-enroll in another course, if space is available.

2. Students must successfully complete 6 hours of behind-the-wheel training. This is typically scheduled once the classroom portion is complete. Students must have their Learners Permit to be scheduled for the Behind-the-wheel Instruction.

3. Students are expected to follow all rules of conduct found in the folder received on the first day of class. Failure to abide by the rules will result in dismissal from class WITH NO REFUND.

4. Driver's Education must be completed on(1) year from class start date.  After 1 year fees apply.  Student records are held for 3 years(COMAR 11.23.02.09).

5. More policies can be located on our "Policy" page.
