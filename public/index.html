Bayside Driving School is Now Bay Country Driving School

Honoring the Legacy, Driving Toward the Future

We are excited to announce that Bay Country Driving School has officially acquired Bayside Driving School. Since 1982, Bayside Driving School has been a trusted name in driver education across Talbot County, and we at Bay Country Driving School are honored to continue its legacy.

Founded by Jack & Beth Morris and later guided by Robin and Zoe Keeter, Bayside Driving School has shaped generations of safe, responsible drivers. Now, under the leadership of Bay Country Driving School, we remain committed to the same high-quality driver education that the community has relied on for decades.

As the Keeters transition into retirement, rest assured that the expert instructors who have trained thousands of students will continue their work at Bay Country Driving School. Our team brings over 100 years of combined experience in behind-the-wheel and classroom instruction, ensuring a seamless transition for new and returning students.

Originally established in Trappe, Bayside Driving School expanded its reach to multiple counties over the years. While the name has changed, Bay Country Driving School continues to focus on providing the best driver education in Talbot County, upholding the family-owned values that made Bayside Driving School a community staple.

We are proud to offer the same trusted training, including access to our private driving range, where students can practice essential skills like parallel parking and two-point-reverse turns in a controlled environment. Our seasoned instructors at Bay Country Driving School are dedicated to fostering a safe, supportive learning atmosphere for all students.
