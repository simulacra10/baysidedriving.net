 Bayside Driving School was established in Trappe in 1982 by the husband and wife team, Jack & Beth Morris, operating out of their home. As the years progressed and demand increased an office was established in Easton. Driver’s Ed classes were extended to three other counties including Caroline, Queen Anne and Dorchester. The Morris family including their three daughters, Samantha, Zoe,and Megan have all helped by putting in their time toward the betterment of the company. In recent years the decision was made to focus on the county that we  originally started in  Talbot county  We are still family owned and operated by Robin and Zoe Keeter. Bayside Driving School’s driving instructors have all been trained and licensed through the MVA’s approved licensing program.

We collectively have over 100 years of experience in Behind-the-wheel instruction as well as classroom instruction. Our personal private driving range offers students an opportunity to practice parallel parking and the two-point-reverse turn in a contained environment for optimal learning. We are confident that our seasoned instructors have the skills and experience to create a safe learning environment for our students. Bayside Driving School has been in business since 1982 and currently has several employees who have worked here for more than 20 years. We are pleased that you have chosen Bayside Driving School and appreciate your business. Buckle Up and Drive Safely!
