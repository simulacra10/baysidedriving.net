Bayside Driving School offers the Driver Improvement Program approved by Maryland MVA.
 
It can be taken as an in-person class OR online program.
 
The online program can only be done if you are over 18.
 
Check your assignment letter from MVA if it says "Assignment to Young Driver Improvement" This will NOT satisfy the MVA requirement. Check your assigment letter again...
 
DIP is usually assigned to:
  • Driver's with 5 or more points on their license
  • Drivers who are on their Provisional license and get  ANY moving violation regardless of PBJ from court
  • Driver's referred by the court system
  • Drivers who voluntarily take it before traffic court
  • Drivers who are revoked or suspended
 
ONLY THOSE CLIENTS THAT PRESENT AN OFFICIAL MVA ASSIGNMENT LETTER or MVA DIRECTIVE  WILL HAVE THEIR COMPLETION OF THE PROGRAM TRANSMITTED TO THEIR DRIVING RECORD.
 
ALL OTHER CLIENTS COMPLETING THE PROGRAM WILL RECEIVE COMPLETION PAPERWORK AND IT IS YOUR REPONSIBILITY TO DISTRIBUTE THE PAPERWORK TO THE APPROPRIATE AGENCY/PERSON.
 
THINGS YOU MUST HAVE FOR IN-PERSON CLASS: 
 
MVA REFERRAL LETTER-If you do not have one call the office
GOVERNMENT ISSUED PHOTO ID
PAYMENT - 75.00 CASH OR MONEY ORDER ONLY- no checks,no cards
